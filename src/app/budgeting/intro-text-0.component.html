Think about your dream. What is it? Is it freedom from a job or boss that you hate? Is it being debt free? Or is it buying a home for your family or traveling around the world? No matter what your dream is, budgeting can help you get there.
And, no, budgeting is not that the occasional glance at your bank account to see how much money you have left. True budgeting is the deliberate process of selecting a budget period, making a plan for your money during that time period, then tracking your income, bills, and expenses on a daily basis as you progress through the budget period in order to understand if you're on track to hit your initial plan.
That is how you move forward financially. That is budgeting!
