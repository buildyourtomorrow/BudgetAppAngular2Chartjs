On the next page, do the following.
In the Assets tab, enter the balances in all your bank, investment, and retirement accounts. Also, enter the value of any property you own (examples - home, car, jewelry)
In the Liabilities tab, enter the amount owed on all debt (make sure to include all personal, car, retirement, student, mortgage, IRS, and mortgage loans)
In the Net Worth tab, a positive (green) number indicates that our assets are more than our liabilities. A negative (red) figure tells us the opposite. Either way, we can directly impact our net worth everyday by spending less than we earn!
